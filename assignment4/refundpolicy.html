Refund Policy

We strive to provide the best laundry services. If you are not satisfied with our service, please review our refund policy below.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Refunds are applicable only for services that have not been delivered as promised or in case of service failure.
  • Requests for refunds must be made within 3 days of service delivery.
  • Refunds are not provided for dissatisfaction due to reasons beyond our control (e.g., pre-existing stains, fabric damage, etc.).

How to Request a Refund

  • Contact our support team at deepz4work@gmail.com with your order details and reason for the refund.
  • Our team will review your request and respond within 2 business days.

Refund Process

  • If approved, refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 7 business days.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once your refund has been processed.
